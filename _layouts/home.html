Welcome to CubingUSA!

This site is to help grow the speedcubing community in the US, and to provide websites for independently-organized Rubik's Cube competitions. If you've been solving that Rubik's Cube for a while, and would like to compete, take a look at the upcoming competitions. If you register for an account, you can find other speedcubers in your area. If you're new to competing, see our Competitor Tutorial so you know what's in store for you when you go to a competition.

About Speedcubing

Speedcubing is solving the Rubik's Cube as fast as you can. In 1982, the first World competition was held in Budapest, Hungary. In 2003, the World Championship returned. Since then, many people from around the world have been competing.
